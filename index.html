Watching a neural network evolve

This showcase exists to display neural networks training in real time.
Each run, 100 cars will be generated and attempt to get as far as possible while avoiding the randomly generated oncoming traffic.
The "brain" and all the neuron activations of the best car will be displayed on the right.
You can automate the training or generate each generation manually. Even when automated you can use the buttons beside the track to save a new best car to reproduce or get rid of the current species.
